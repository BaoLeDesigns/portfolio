Artist Summary

I'm a San Jose–based 3D artist and SJSU BFA Digital Media Arts graduate specializing in:

• Game-ready environment art
• Modular asset props
• Atmospheric lighting & mood
• Realistic product visualization
• Photogrammetry workflows

I focus on clean topology, efficient UVs, and integrating PBR materials for real-time projects. I've contributed to multiple solo and collaborative game projects, working across modeling, level design, lighting, and asset optimization.

I communicate clearly, iterate quickly, and enjoy fast-paced environments where ideas evolve through feedback. I'm pursuing opportunities in environment art, visualization, and 3D generalist roles.
