Bao Le

A 21-year-old Digital Media Arts student at San Jose State University, was born in Bac Lieu, Vietnam, and raised in San Jose, California. Growing up in Silicon Valley, he developed a passion for technology early on. Now specializing in 3D environment art and Game Design, he aims to contribute to game development by creating immersive digital worlds and fun and rewarding game systems.
